Why should I study with Joseph Ritchie?


Joseph offers private lessons to middle school and high school low brass students. Joseph teaches each of his students how to become independent learners and strong musicians. Joseph's students have been selected to participate in region and state level bands in various states. Many of Joseph's students have also recieved collegiate music scholarships.
